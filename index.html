I fell in love with the enormity of space as a little girl.
Living in the middle of a civil war, the land below wasn't as peaceful as the skies above. Terrorists bombed the nearby streets and I spent my childhood indoors, wrapped within my mother's arms, watching the night sky. The unfamiliarity of space became my escape from the all-to-familiar reality below.

I'm far away now from the terrors of the civil war, but my love for the night sky has remained close-by. With the endless possibilities that space holds, it's my love for exploration and adventure that brings me back to space every single night.
